Wire-Cutting Electric Discharge Machine Cooling Liquid/Gel


Product Name: Wire-Cutting Electric Discharge Machine Cooling Liquid/Gel

Proportion: Mix the cooling liquid with running water in a ratio of 1:40 to 50 (avoid using hard water, such as well water or groundwater).

Ingredients: This is an environmentally friendly product made from green ingredients that are safe for both users and the environment.

Application Range: Suitable for all types of wire-cutting electric discharge machines (EDM). It can also be used as a cooling liquid in metal-cutting processes.

Features:

  • Excellent Cleaning Performance: This product offers outstanding cleaning capabilities, especially in high-thickness and multi-cut operations.
  • Fast Dissolution: The liquid dissolves quickly in water, making it easy to use and convenient to handle.
  • High Efficiency: Under heavy operational conditions, such as high energy (average processing current over 5A), it provides significant benefits over traditional emulsified oils. It improves cutting efficiency by 30%, enhances surface finish by half a grade, reduces electrode wire loss by 50%, and significantly extends the life of molybdenum wire.
  • Uniform Finish: The product ensures a uniform, white surface finish without the black-and-white stripes commonly seen when cleaned with kerosene.
  • Improved Workpiece Handling: The workpiece is easy to remove and clean, and the workbench stays clean throughout the process.
  • Cutting Capability: Ideal for cutting die steel up to 500mm in thickness and suitable for multiple cuts.
  • Shelf Life: 12 months from the date of manufacture.

Precautions:

  • The product appears as yellow ointment with a slight amount of water. Stir and mix thoroughly before use. After dilution, the liquid turns a light milky white color.
  • When replenishing the liquid, ensure that the correct proportion of ointment and water is used.
  • To avoid breaking the molybdenum wire, use a magnet to hold the workpiece before processing is completed.
