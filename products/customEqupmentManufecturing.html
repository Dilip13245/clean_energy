Custome Equpment Manufacturing

At Clean Clean Energy, we specialize in the design, engineering, and manufacturing of custom equipment tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients. Whether you're looking for specialized machinery, components, or fully integrated systems, our team has the expertise and resources to deliver high-quality solutions that optimize your operations. We take pride in our ability to create equipment that is not only custom-built to your specifications but also engineered for reliability, efficiency, and performance.

Our Custom Equipment Manufacturing Solutions:


  1. Custom Equipment Design: We begin each project by working closely with you to understand the specific requirements of your operation. Our engineering team uses advanced design software and tools to create equipment that meets your exact specifications. From the conceptual phase through detailed design, we ensure that each piece of equipment is optimized for performance, durability, and ease of use.
  2. Prototype Development: Before moving to full-scale production, we offer prototype development to validate the design and functionality of the custom equipment. Our team builds a working model of the equipment that allows you to evaluate performance, identify potential issues, and make any necessary adjustments. This iterative approach ensures that the final product meets your expectations and performs as intended in real-world applications.
  3. Precision Machining and Fabrication: Once the design is finalized, we proceed with precision machining and fabrication. Using state-of-the-art CNC machines and skilled craftsmanship, we produce high-precision parts and components that fit together perfectly. Our fabrication services include welding, cutting, bending, and assembly, ensuring that each piece of custom equipment is constructed to the highest quality standards.
  4. Material Selection: We carefully select materials that meet the demands of your specific application, ensuring the right balance of strength, durability, and corrosion resistance. Whether you need stainless steel, aluminum, high-strength alloys, or specialty materials, we provide the best material options for your equipment to ensure it performs optimally in your environment.
  5. Electrical and Control Systems Integration: For equipment that requires electrical systems, automation, or control features, we integrate cutting-edge control systems, PLCs (Programmable Logic Controllers), and other electrical components to provide seamless operation. Whether you need simple on/off control or complex feedback loops, we ensure that your equipment operates efficiently and reliably. We can also incorporate sensors, actuators, and other automation elements for enhanced performance.
  6. Assembly and Integration: After all components are fabricated, we proceed with the mechanical and electrical assembly of the custom equipment. Our team ensures that every part is installed and integrated correctly, including hydraulic, pneumatic, and electrical systems. We handle all aspects of equipment assembly, ensuring that it functions smoothly and meets the required specifications.
  7. Testing and Quality Assurance: We conduct comprehensive testing to ensure that the custom equipment operates as intended. This includes functional testing, load testing, safety checks, and performance evaluations. We also conduct thorough inspections to verify that the equipment meets all quality standards and regulatory requirements. Any necessary adjustments are made to ensure optimal performance before delivery.
  8. Custom Modifications and Upgrades: In addition to building new custom equipment, we also offer modification and upgrade services for existing machinery. If your equipment needs enhancements or improvements to meet evolving operational needs, we provide tailored solutions to modify and optimize your machinery, extending its useful life and improving its efficiency.
  9. Packaging, Shipping, and Installation: Once your custom equipment has passed all testing phases, we prepare it for delivery. We handle all packaging, skidding, and crating to ensure that the equipment arrives safely at your location. Our installation team is available to ensure that the equipment is properly installed and integrated into your operations. We work closely with your team to ensure a smooth and seamless installation process.
  10. Ongoing Support and Maintenance: We offer ongoing support to ensure the continued operation and efficiency of your custom equipment. Our team is available for troubleshooting, repairs, and maintenance services. We also provide training for your staff to ensure they are familiar with the operation and maintenance of the equipment, reducing the risk of downtime and enhancing productivity.
