Protect Water Tank & Cover

Climate Protect Water Tank Jacket & Cover is an innovative, one-piece solution developed by us to optimize water temperature and protect your water tank from various environmental factors. This versatile cover ensures that your water stays at a safe temperature, regardless of extreme weather conditions. It protects your tank from dust, insects, and animals like monkeys while also keeping it securely closed. Additionally, it prevents hazardous hot water during the summer, safeguarding your family's health.

Designed to fit various inlet, outlet, and overflow pipes, the cover includes both side and top protection to maintain water temperature in fluctuating weather conditions. This jacket is perfect for regions with peak summer temperatures ranging from 36°C to 48°C and winter temperatures between 10°C to 20°C, optimizing water temperature by 6°C to 8°C compared to the external environment.

The reflective surface reflects heat and UV rays, providing insulation and maintaining safe water temperature, even in the hottest summer or coldest winter. The cover is easy to install by one person within 5 minutes, fitting any pre-installed water tank without the need to remove pipe fittings.

Key Features:

  • Two layers of UV protection to prevent damage and maintain water quality.
  • Simple installation—can be done by anyone, no tools required.
  • Option to unfold during the monsoon season for longer life (optional).
  • Custom-designed to fit various inlet/outlet and overflow pipes, with a top cover to maintain ideal water temperature.
  • Helps prevent health issues caused by sun exposure and UV rays.
  • Reduces damage to RO filters and other equipment due to hot water exposure.
  • Significant savings on electricity bills by reducing the need for additional water heating.
  • Extends the lifespan of your water tank.
  • 2 years brand warranty.
