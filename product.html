Our Product Range

At Clean Energy, we specialize in a comprehensive range of products tailored to meet the needs of various industrial sectors. Our offerings include:

  • EDM Wire Cutting Machines & Parts: We provide high-precision EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) wire cutting machines, along with essential components and accessories. In addition, we offer EDM wire cut coolant gel, which enhances the cutting process by reducing friction, extending tool life, and improving overall efficiency.
  • Industrial Automation Products: Our industrial automation solutions are designed to streamline production processes, enhance operational efficiency, and reduce costs. We provide a variety of automation products that improve the performance of manufacturing systems, from sensors to control systems.
  • Industrial Mechanical Products: We offer a wide range of durable and high-performance mechanical products that are essential for industries requiring robust machinery and components for their operations.
  • Industrial Laser Products: Our industrial-grade laser systems are engineered for precision cutting, marking, and engraving applications. We ensure that each laser product meets the highest industry standards, providing reliability and accuracy.
  • Water Tank Covers: Clean Energy manufactures durable and protective water tank covers that ensure the safety and cleanliness of water storage systems, guarding against contaminants and environmental conditions.
  • Heat Exchangers: Our high-quality heat exchangers are designed for efficient heat transfer, ensuring optimal performance in a wide range of industrial applications, from HVAC systems to power generation.
  • Solar Panels: We are proud to contribute to sustainable energy solutions by offering high-efficiency solar panels. These panels are ideal for residential, commercial, and industrial applications, helping reduce energy costs and environmental impact.
  • Gas Regulators: Clean Energy provides precise and reliable gas regulators that are essential for safely controlling gas flow in industrial, commercial, and residential settings.
