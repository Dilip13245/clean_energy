About Us

Welcome to Clean Energy, a leading Import & Export Trading Company based in Veraval, Gujarat, India. With a dedication to excellence, we serve as a trusted supplier of high-quality and cost-effective products across diverse industries. Our company is committed to providing reliable solutions for both domestic and international markets, ensuring that our clients have access to top-tier products and services at competitive prices.

Our Product Range

At Clean Energy, we specialize in a comprehensive range of products tailored to meet the needs of various industrial sectors. Our offerings include:

  • EDM Wire Cutting Machines & Parts: We provide high-precision EDM (Electrical Discharge Machining) wire cutting machines, along with essential components and accessories. In addition, we offer EDM wire cut coolant gel, which enhances the cutting process by reducing friction, extending tool life, and improving overall efficiency.
  • Industrial Automation Products: Our industrial automation solutions are designed to streamline production processes, enhance operational efficiency, and reduce costs. We provide a variety of automation products that improve the performance of manufacturing systems, from sensors to control systems.
  • Industrial Mechanical Products: We offer a wide range of durable and high-performance mechanical products that are essential for industries requiring robust machinery and components for their operations.
  • Industrial Laser Products: Our industrial-grade laser systems are engineered for precision cutting, marking, and engraving applications. We ensure that each laser product meets the highest industry standards, providing reliability and accuracy.
  • Water Tank Covers: Clean Energy manufactures durable and protective water tank covers that ensure the safety and cleanliness of water storage systems, guarding against contaminants and environmental conditions.
  • Heat Exchangers: Our high-quality heat exchangers are designed for efficient heat transfer, ensuring optimal performance in a wide range of industrial applications, from HVAC systems to power generation.
  • Solar Panels: We are proud to contribute to sustainable energy solutions by offering high-efficiency solar panels. These panels are ideal for residential, commercial, and industrial applications, helping reduce energy costs and environmental impact.
  • Gas Regulators: Clean Energy provides precise and reliable gas regulators that are essential for safely controlling gas flow in industrial, commercial, and residential settings.

Our Manufacturing Capabilities

In addition to trading, we also excel in the manufacturing of advanced machinery and equipment. Some of our notable manufactured products include:

  • Washing Brush Multi-Head Drilling Machines: Our specially designed multi-head drilling machines offer exceptional performance for a variety of industrial applications, improving speed and accuracy in manufacturing processes.
  • Digital Printing Machines: Clean Energy manufactures high-quality digital printing machines that deliver superior print resolution and color accuracy for modern printing applications.
  • Textile Air Jet Rid Machines: We design and produce textile air jet rid machines that enhance textile production processes, ensuring high-quality fabric output with improved efficiency.
  • Special Purpose Machines (SPMs): We offer custom-designed special-purpose machines tailored to meet specific industrial requirements. These machines are crafted to address unique challenges in manufacturing, delivering enhanced productivity and precision.

Repair and Maintenance Services

At Clean Energy, we understand that equipment downtime can be costly for industries. Therefore, we provide expert repair services for various types of machinery and laser sources. Our experienced technicians specialize in repairing:

  • Laser Sources: We offer repairs for a wide range of laser sources, including Max Laser Source, Raycus Laser Source, IPG, and other popular models.
  • Servo Motors & Drivers: We provide repair and maintenance services for servo motors and drivers from renowned manufacturers such as Siemens, Mitsubishi, Fanuc, and more.
  • CNC and VMC Controllers: Our technicians are skilled in repairing and servicing CNC (Computer Numerical Control) and VMC (Vertical Machining Center) controllers, including models from industry leaders like Fanuc, Mitsubishi, Siemens, and Schneider.
