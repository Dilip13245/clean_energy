Repair and Maintenance Service Details

We offer a broad range of repair and maintenance services designed to keep your equipment, machinery, or property running smoothly. Whether you're dealing with a minor issue or a major breakdown, our experienced technicians are here to help.

1. Equipment Repair & Maintenance

Repair Services: Our skilled technicians are trained to repair all types of equipment, from household appliances to heavy industrial machinery.

  • Fault diagnosis
  • Component replacement
  • System calibration
  • Software troubleshooting (if applicable)

Maintenance Services: Regular maintenance is essential for preventing breakdowns and extending the lifespan of your equipment.

  • Preventive maintenance schedules
  • Routine inspections
  • Cleaning and lubrication
  • Parts replacement
2. Laser Source Repairing

Laser Systems Repair: Specialized repair services for laser sources used in various industries, including medical devices, manufacturing, and research labs.

  • Laser module diagnostics and repairs
  • Power supply testing and component replacement
  • Optics and alignment adjustments
  • Preventive maintenance for longevity
3. Electronic Circuit Repairing

Circuit Board Diagnostics and Repair: We specialize in repairing and restoring electronic circuit boards for consumer electronics, industrial machinery, and more.

  • Component testing and replacement (capacitors, resistors, ICs, etc.)
  • Soldering and reflow services
  • Short-circuit and trace repairs
  • Customized circuit modifications and upgrades
Third-Party Installation Services Details

We offer comprehensive installation solutions for various equipment and systems, from renewable energy solutions to industrial machinery and essential home utilities. Below are some of the key services we provide:

Solar Installation Services

Residential and Commercial Solar Systems: We provide professional installation of solar panels for both residential and commercial properties. Our team ensures the efficient setup and integration of solar power systems, helping you reduce energy bills and promote sustainability.

  • Site Assessment: Thorough inspection to determine the best installation location.
  • Panel Mounting: Secure mounting of solar panels with the correct angle and orientation.
  • Electrical Connection: Proper wiring, inverter setup, and grid connection.
  • System Testing: Post-installation testing to ensure peak performance.
  • Solar Battery Storage Systems: Along with solar panels, we can install energy storage solutions, allowing you to store excess energy for use during non-sunny periods, increasing your system's efficiency.
Machinery Installation

Industrial Machinery: From manufacturing machines to factory equipment, we handle the complete installation of heavy machinery. Our technicians ensure that all components are properly aligned and functional.

  • Machine Setup: Accurate positioning, leveling, and securing of machines.
  • Power and Control Connections: Electrical and mechanical connections to ensure safe operation.
  • Testing and Calibration: Thorough testing and calibration of machinery for optimal performance.
  • Office and Commercial Equipment: For offices and commercial spaces, we install various types of equipment such as printers, copiers, air conditioning units, and more.
  • Placement and Configuration: Strategically placing equipment for optimal functionality and user accessibility.
  • Wiring and Connectivity: Ensuring proper electrical and data connections.
Gas Regulator Installation for Boilers

Boiler Gas Regulator Installation: Proper installation of gas regulators for boilers is critical for safe and efficient operation. Our technicians ensure that your system is up to code and works efficiently.

  • Pressure Regulation Setup: Correct installation of regulators to manage the flow and pressure of gas to the boiler.
  • Leak Testing: Comprehensive testing for gas leaks to ensure safety.
  • System Integration: Properly integrating the regulator into your existing boiler system.
  • Routine Maintenance & Safety Checks: We also offer regular maintenance services to ensure the gas regulators remain in top condition, preventing malfunctions or safety hazards.
